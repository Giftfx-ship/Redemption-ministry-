Welcome to Redemption Ministries Warri

We are a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to proclaiming the message of redemption, restoring lives through the Word of God, and raising believers who walk in purpose, power, and truth.

About the Ministry

Redemption Ministries Warri exists to proclaim the message of redemption through the finished work of Christ. We believe in the authority of the Bible, the power of prayer, and the work of the Holy Spirit.

Our ministry is committed to evangelism, discipleship, and raising leaders who will impact their generation for Christ.

Our Vision

To raise a redeemed people who understand their identity in Christ and manifest the kingdom of God on earth.

Our Mission

  • Preach the gospel of Jesus Christ
  • Teach sound biblical doctrine
  • Raise disciples and leaders
  • Impact lives and communities
