About Redemption Ministries

Redemption Ministries is a Christ-centered ministry committed to preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ, restoring lives, and raising believers who walk in purpose, power, and truth.

Our services are marked by heartfelt worship, sound biblical teaching, prayer, and the tangible presence of God. We are a family built on faith, love, and truth.

Our Vision

To raise a redeemed generation that reflects Christ in character, authority, and purpose.

Our Mission

  • To preach the undiluted Word of God
  • To disciple believers into spiritual maturity
  • To restore lives through faith and truth
  • To impact communities with the love of Christ
